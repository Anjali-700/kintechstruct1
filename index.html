Dual-Mode Analysis Platform

Our suite combines two powerful, specialized applications into one package, engineered for researchers, students, and professionals in proteomics and enzymology.

  • Advanced Protein Sequence Analysis
  • Enzyme Kinetics Database Analyzer
  • Integrated Statistical & Plotting Tools
  • 3D Structure Fetching & Visualization

Powerful Research Features

Protein Sequence Analysis

Calculate physicochemical properties: Molecular Weight, pI, Charge, Instability Index, Half-life, and more.

3D Structure Analysis

Fetch PDB structures directly from the RCSB database. View and interact with 3D models in your browser via py3Dmol.

Protein Plotting

Generate key plots including Ramachandran plots, Hydrophobicity profiles, and Amino Acid Composition charts.

Kinetics Database

Load, search, and filter large enzyme kinetics datasets (kcat, Km, ΔΔG). Search by EC number, organism, or substrate.

Kinetics Visualization

Create 10+ publication-ready plots: kcat/Km comparisons, ΔΔG distributions, correlation matrices, and more.

Statistical Analysis

Perform descriptive statistics, correlation analysis, regression, and normality tests on your enzyme kinetics data.
